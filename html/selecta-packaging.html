Client

|   Unilever Philippines - Selecta

Task

|   Refresh the packaging while staying recognizably Selecta

The challenge was to refresh the Bestseller line packaging while making sure it was still recognizably ‘Selecta’.

I adapted a cleaner and sharper look for the packaging- significantly less typefaces used, brighter colors, and a well art directed placement of the ingredients for a cleaner look- even with a glance from a far, one will still be able to see both its likeness and difference to the previous prackaging design.
